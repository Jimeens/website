Personal study

At certain times of the day, I search for articles (arXiv), books, and lecture notes on the areas I am interested in and plan to pursue in my master's/doctorate/life.

Coffee moment

At certain times of the day, I seek to go to the best place in the physics institute (Hackerspace) to have coffee with some friends and chat about life.
